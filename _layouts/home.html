Embedded Linux means alot of things to many people. For the purpose of this site, it means the Linux kernel plus the root file-system that comprise a custom Linux distribution.

Here, I document how a custom Linux distribution is put together and I build one from scratch without the use of build tools such as Buildroot or Yocto.
